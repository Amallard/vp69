Welcome Aboard to Patrol Squadron SIX NINE (VP-69)!

　

ABOUT

VP-69 is a tenant command aboard Naval Air Station Whidbey Island, outside the town of Oak Harbor, WA. Oak Harbor is a small community about 70 miles north of Seattle and about 90 miles south of Vancouver, British Columbia. There are numerous schools, churches, and recreational activities available in the local area. Whidbey Island's close proximity to both Mt. Vernon and Bellingham allows access to two malls and many retail establishments within a one to two-hour commute.

There are numerous things to do in Oak Harbor. Fishing, camping, and skiing are just a few fun things that many Sailors find make life here so enjoyable. Our NAS Whidbey Island MWR is one of the best in the Navy and offers numerous activities and discounted tickets for recreational fun.
